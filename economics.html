Economics: Hybrid Model (Option C)

A stable base + scalable upside: per-driver subscription plus per-ride platform fee. Transparent, predictable, and fair.

Hybrid pricing

  • $20 / active driver / month – platform access + core SaaS operations.
  • $0.25 / completed ride – usage-based fee aligned to load and value delivered.
  • Governance: cap the platform take-rate (e.g., ≤ 5%) to protect drivers.
Why it works: predictable baseline + growth capture without squeezing drivers.

You can tune both levers (driver fee and ride fee) by market and by operator tier.

What the fee funds

  • Platform uptime and upgrades
  • Edge API + service portal
  • Event processing and analytics readiness
  • Support and incident response

Operators pay the platform; drivers keep the majority of fare revenue.
