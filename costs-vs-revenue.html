Costs vs Revenue

SaaS remains profitable when platform revenue scales faster than support and platform operations.

Sustainability

  • Revenue scales with drivers and rides.
  • Support + platform ops scale slower than ride volume.
  • Margins improve with adoption and multi-tenant reuse.

This chart is illustrative. Use your workbook to plug in exact support and staffing assumptions.

Operating boundaries

  • Platform team: uptime, upgrades, data readiness.
  • Stakeholders: market ops, driver/rider support, pricing decisions.
