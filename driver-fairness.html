Driver Fairness (70–80%)

A driver-first platform is sustainable when governance and pricing guardrails are explicit.

Fairness goals

  • Drivers keep 70–80% of gross fares.
  • Operator keeps transparent margin for operations.
  • SaaS fee is capped and decoupled from surge manipulation.
Guardrail: platform fees are capped and never exceed a fixed percentage of fares.

Example split

Recipient% of fare
Driver70–80%
Operator15–25%
SaaS Platform2–5%

Operators can tune the split. The platform stays profitable due to scalable cloud economics and low take-rate.

Why this remains profitable

  • Fees are aligned to usage (rides, active drivers).
  • Support costs scale slower than ride volume.
  • Analytics/warehouse can be premium tiers at scale.
