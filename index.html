Rideshare SaaS Platform

A fair, scalable rideshare SaaS platform for operators—designed so drivers keep 70–80% while the platform remains profitable.

SaaS-firstDriver-first economicsDFW-readyEvent-driven

What this is

  • SaaS platform for rideshare operators (we provide technology and upgrades—not the rideshare business operations).
  • Fair economics with explicit guardrails: drivers keep 70–80% of fare revenue.
  • Hybrid pricing (recommended): per-driver subscription + per-ride fee.
  • Cloud-native: microservices, event-driven, analytics-ready.
Positioning: We don’t compete as a marketplace. We enable operators with transparent, sustainable infrastructure.
